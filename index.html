Welcome to Siddhi Jutex

Your Trusted Partner in Sustainable Packaging

Emerging Manufacturer Since 2024

Siddhi Jutex Creative Enterprises is a Kolkata-based enterprise established in 2024, specializing in the manufacturing of premium quality jute and eco-friendly packaging products for wholesale and export markets. Despite being a new-age company, we are driven by modern innovation, strong business ethics, and a commitment to excellence.

Located in the industrial hub of Kolkata, we leverage advanced manufacturing techniques and skilled craftsmanship to produce durable jute bags, hessian cloth, and customized industrial packaging solutions that meet national and international quality standards.

We are committed to sustainability, ethical practices, and delivering exceptional value to our clients worldwide.

Our Vision & Mission

Building a Sustainable Future for India

Our vision is to become one of India’s most trusted manufacturers of eco-friendly jute products, promoting sustainable practices and reducing plastic usage across the country. We are committed to supporting the “Make in India” initiative by delivering high-quality, locally manufactured packaging solutions.

We believe in responsible sourcing, ethical business practices, and contributing towards a greener and cleaner India.
